OVERVIEW

THE PROBLEM

As university students, we find ourselves in a common frustrating situation of arriving at the library, only to discover that all the seats were taken. This recurring issue not only leaves us tired and unmotivated to study, but also wastes precious time. Our team determined that there had to be a better way to address this problem and make the library experience more efficient for Unimelb students.

THE SOLUTION

This sparked the idea for OptiMate, a real-time seat availability tracker for libraries allowing students to make informed decisions about where to study and optimize their time at university. With OptiMate, students can easily check the availability of seats in their preferred library before heading out, saving them valuable time and reducing stress.

THE PROTOTYPE

EASY ACCESS TO DESIRED LIBRARY

Each library is stored as its own card and can be accessed through the landing page through scrolling down, typing in the search bar or filtering search based on key ammenities available.

NARROW DOWN THE SEAT YOU WANT

Within each library, view real time the number of seats available on each level and decide where to go. Further filtering options include seat type, noise level and power outlets availability.

BOOSTING PRODUCTIVITY & MOTIVATION

A simple pomodoro timer and to-do list page available, allowing users to input tasks and mark them off as they finish it while seeing the timer at the same time. Furthermore, a study streak feature is present, a fun way to let users track their progress and stay motivated.
