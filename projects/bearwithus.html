OVERVIEW

This project aims to analyse & identify areas for improvement in the usability of Bear With Us (March 2025 version)

THE PROBLEM

In my chosen website, ‘Bear With Us’, I have identified usability problems in navigation, colour contrast, and spacing between elements. Additionally, the website prioritises promotion of bookings, and had little indication that products are availabe for purchase.

THE GOAL

My goal for this project build a clear wireframe for the e-commerce aspect of the website. I will also be establishing a colour palette that provides more contrast (while retaining some original colours) and restructure the homepage to emphasize the main functionailites of the website (booking & shopping)

WEBSITE ANALYSIS

THE PROTOTYPE

HOME PAGE WITH CLEAR PURPOSE

Find out what services are offered (shopping and booking) within one scroll.

PRODUCT PAGE WITH SELECTABLE SIZES

The page now has a cleaner look compared to previously where is item size is a separate product, creating"duplicate" items. In my final design, the price would increase with item size.

A CLEAR CHECKING OUT WEBFLOW

I have split the payment processing into four different segment, allowing users to complete four small tasks rather than fill out a big form. With each category displayed on top, users can go back and check their details again if needed. Input boxes follow a standard layout, which can be intituitive to users.
